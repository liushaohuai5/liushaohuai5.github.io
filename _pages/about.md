I am a second-year PhD student at Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, Texas A&M University, supervised by [Prof. Le Xie](https://xiele00.github.io/) and co-advised by [Prof. Chao Tian](https://engineering.tamu.edu/electrical/profiles/tian-chao.html).

I received my master's degree under the supervision of [Prof. Yang Gao](https://yang-gao.weebly.com/) at [Institute of Interdisciplinary Information Science](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/). 

I got my bachelor's degree at Department of Electrical Engineering, working with [Prof. Qirong Jiang](https://www.eea.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/faculties/qrjiang.htm) and [Prof. Haiwang Zhong](https://www.eea.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/faculties/zhonghw.htm).

My research interests concentrate on RL, including subtopics across sample-efficient, constraint-aware, agentic, as well as intersections with power systems and robotics. 

I was a student athlete for over 15 years, including services for YALI High School, Tsinghua University Men's Soccer Team. A MF and also fan of Messi, Barcelona, and Argentina.
